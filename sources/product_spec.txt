Product Specification: NovaAssist Voice Companion, v2.1

Overview
NovaAssist is a voice-enabled AI assistant for small business owners, designed to
handle appointment scheduling, customer FAQs, and basic order tracking through
natural spoken conversation.

Core Features
The assistant supports speech-to-text transcription with under 400ms latency using
a streaming ASR pipeline. Responses are generated using a fine-tuned language model
and converted back to speech using a low-latency TTS engine. The system supports
English and Hindi at launch, with Kannada and Tamil planned for v2.2.

Architecture
NovaAssist runs on a three-stage pipeline: audio capture and transcription, intent
classification and response generation, and speech synthesis. Each stage is
independently scalable and communicates over a lightweight message queue. Average
end-to-end response time in testing was 1.8 seconds.

Hardware Requirements
The assistant is designed to run on a standard business tablet or smartphone with
at least 2GB of available RAM. No specialized hardware is required for the
customer-facing device; heavier processing occurs on backend servers.

Pricing Tiers
The Starter tier costs $29/month and supports up to 500 conversations. The Growth
tier costs $79/month for up to 3000 conversations and adds calendar integration.
The Enterprise tier is custom-priced and includes dedicated support and on-premise
deployment options.

Known Limitations
The current version struggles with heavy background noise environments, showing a
12% increase in transcription errors in noisy settings during internal testing.
Multi-speaker conversations are not yet supported; the assistant assumes a single
speaker per session. These issues are targeted for resolution in v2.2, expected in
Q1 2027.

Support
Enterprise customers receive a dedicated account manager and 24/7 technical support.
Starter and Growth tier customers receive email support with a 48-hour response
target during business days.
